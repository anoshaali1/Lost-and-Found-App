Overview of the Lost and Found App

The Lost and Found App is a mobile and web-based application designed to help users efficiently report, search for, and recover lost items. The app provides a centralized digital platform where individuals can post details of lost or found belongings and connect with others who may have relevant information.

Users who lose an item can create a lost report by providing item descriptions, images, location, and date. Similarly, users who find an item can submit a found report with matching details. The system intelligently organizes and displays posts, making it easier to identify potential matches.

The app aims to reduce the time, effort, and stress involved in recovering lost items by promoting community participation and real-time communication. It can be used in environments such as universities, offices, public places, transport stations, and cities.

Overall, the Lost and Found App enhances trust, collaboration, and efficiency by leveraging technology to reunite people with their belongings.
